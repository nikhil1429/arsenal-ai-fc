# PART I — THE PITCH

## 1. What it is, in one breath

**Arsenal AI FC is a cognitive prosthesis** — a personal, multi-agent AI system that carries the executive functions one specific human brain under-supplies (initiation, working memory, time-sense, task-switching), so that his consistency, not his condition, decides the outcome. It is built as a **football club**: the human is the captain (#14) and the only irreplaceable organ; everything else is the rest of the body, organized to circulate one thing — *the rep* (a unit of studied, self-tested work).

It has two AI brains behind one voice (Claude for judgment, Gemini for vision and bulk), a real-time spoken coach you can argue with, a self-tuning overnight scheduler, and a constitution of humane laws enforced **in code, not in prompts**. Its deepest engineering conviction is knowing what it must *refuse* to do.

**The one-line version:** *An AI that wastes nothing you generate and loses nothing you are — and rewrites itself, one reversible gene at a time, around the measured shape of your one specific mind.*

## 2. The problem (three layers deep)

1. **The executive-function problem (the real why).** The builder, Nikhil, has diagnosed, medicated **ADHD-PI** (predominantly inattentive). The functions his cortex under-supplies are *precisely* the ones this loop is built to carry. This isn't a productivity app; it's a prosthesis. The framing the system uses on itself: *"A prosthetic leg does not make you faster than a sprinter. It makes you whole."* It makes him **un-taxed** — then his own consistency does the rest.

2. **The signal-waste problem.** His richest emissions were dying on the vine — 112 captured confusions were *write-only*, thoughts died "on staircases," the same misunderstanding repeated because nothing ever scheduled the rematch. The organism's single job: **capture the signal, and close the loop on it.**

3. **The procrastination problem (the honest scar).** The system exists partly *because* ~12 hours went into designing it with near-zero shipping. For an ADHD-PI builder, elaborate system-building is a seductive, high-dopamine form of procrastination. So the machine has an **anti-procrastination law aimed at itself**: *"When in doubt: do a mock, ship a feature — don't add an agent."* It is designed, above all, to end its own overbuilding.

## 3. Who it's for (the user, honestly)

- **Nikhil Panwar** — DTU Math & Computing grad, ~4 years finance-operations at Zomato/Blinkit (a genuine fintech domain moat), training to land an **AI Product Engineer role in India, ₹20–25 LPA band, 2026.**
- His proof-of-work is a shipped **FinOps Copilot** (invoice-intelligence tool, notebook → live API + eval harness) with a *real user on real Blinkit data* — "not a toy demo."
- The machine trains him for a specific, researched interview target (see §11, The DOSSIER) and guards a specific, documented failure mode: **burnout** (his #1 risk) and **procrastination-by-building** (his #2).

**Why this matters for an investor:** the user is not a hypothetical. Every mechanism was built against a real person's measured psychology, a real goal, and real failure modes. That specificity is the product's soul — and, as §14 argues, its moat.

## 4. Why "an organism" and why a football club

An earlier version of this plan called for "33 agents on 33 timers." The insight that reframed everything: *a 4-slot working-memory brain cannot debug 33 timers.* So it became **one body**, legible as a scoreboard — with the human as the **heart**, not the user, not the janitor. The defining property is that **every arrow is closed**: he emits a rep → the loop learns → the loop changes → the changed loop changes what lands in front of him tomorrow → he changes. Three nested clocks: **the rep · the day · the season.**

The football-club skin is **load-bearing, not decoration** — the builder is an obsessive Arsenal/Arteta fan, and "rivalry is genuinely the currency this brain spends." The rival is always **kal-wala-Nikhil** (yesterday-you) — a race you cannot demoralizingly lose. Every mechanic maps to a real, documented Arteta/Pep principle (a hard rule forbids fabricating a quote). For this one user, the skin does real cognitive work; for anyone else it would be theming.

## 5. The superpowers (the pitch bullets)

1. **Two separate AI billing pools, one voice.** Claude (Max subscription) is treated as scarce judgment capital; Gemini (free tier) does vision and bulk; deterministic code does all math for **zero tokens**. Marginal cost of running the whole machine: **≈ $0.** No API key is ever set — a hard, code-enforced $100 ceiling.
2. **A real-time spoken coach (The Dugout).** Sub-second, interruptible voice — you *argue* with it. It reaches into your live data mid-sentence (15 tools), runs voice drills, stages rematches, files your evening ritual on your spoken word, and even watches your paper/screen through the camera. Built on the free Gemini Live tier with the wire-level bugs empirically solved (§10).
3. **A hot brain that gets sharper while you sleep.** An overnight scheduler deliberately spends the plan's idle capacity — writing tomorrow's coaching cartridge, mining today's conversations into curriculum, rendering art — and self-tunes to the plan's true, unpublished limits.
4. **Earned proactivity (the shadow engine).** The machine is not allowed to interrupt you until it has *silently proven* an interruption would have helped (measured hit-rate) **and** you've ratified it by voice. Restraint as a feature.
5. **A calibration engine that finds your unknown-unknowns.** Every answer is pre-committed with a gut-word (*knew/shaky/guessed*); the machine measures where you were *confident and wrong* — the single most dangerous cell in learning.
6. **Media that makes itself.** Daily posters (fully automatic), 90-second spoken team-talks, and one-click season films — the motivation layer for a brain that thinks in pictures.
7. **A constitution enforced in code.** Win-only voicing, no shame, no streaks, no hype, no calendar pressure, a hard medical boundary — every one is a code path with a passing test, not a line in a prompt an LLM could ignore.

**Two engineering ideas hold the whole thing up:**
- **The single-writer state bus.** The git repo (the "dressing room") is the only shared memory. Every state file has *exactly one writer*; commits are the audit log. It's centralized-by-design (research cited in the vision: independent agents amplify errors ~17× vs ~4.4× centralized; optimal cluster is 3–4). Only the Manager reconciles.
- **AI proposes · code validates · human approves.** The LLM is used *only* for semantic, unbounded judgment. All math, thresholds, and validation are deterministic code. The referees are code, not LLMs — which is itself the "#1 senior signal" the interview target rewards (knowing where *not* to use an LLM).

## 8. The four verbs (the entire human interface)

The captain never "operates" the machine. He touches it through **four verbs plus one honest fifth**:
- **PASTE** — drop a study session's reps in (the blood).
- **SOLVE** — do the drills it set.
- **BOLO** — speak a concept aloud (see Glossary; the interview-defense muscle).
- **COPY-BACK** — confirm/correct what it proposes.
- **THROW-IN** *(the fifth)* — fire any stray thought from the phone; it lands verbatim, waits for evening, never counts against him.

*"You never tend it. You study, you ship, you speak."*

## 9. The organs, one by one (A to Z)

### THE AFFERENT NERVE (how signal gets in)

**`capture.mjs` — the shared capture layer (Agent #0).** The turnstile: the *only* hand that writes reps into `reps_log.jsonl`. Every rep is a validated 13-field line: timestamp, surface (gem/colab), track (concept/skill), concept (canonicalized against a registry), axis (a–i), question, **confidence (knew/shaky/guessed)**, correct, latency, and more. Strict validation; malformed reps are *rejected, never guessed*. Unknown concepts are logged (flagged `unregistered`) rather than dropped. This single-writer discipline is the architectural safety guarantee everything downstream depends on.

**`throwin.mjs` — the fifth verb.** Polls a private ntfy channel so a thought texted from the phone lands verbatim in `loose_balls.jsonl` with `routed:false`. **Iron laws in code:** verbatim byte-for-byte, never counts as a usage metric, and (a fix found in live testing) it drops the organism's *own* pushes so the machine never re-ingests its own voice as your thought.

**`mirror.mjs` — the capsule mirror.** Pulls the canonical learning capsules (the durable knowledge units, stored in a GitHub Gist) into a local read-only copy, with a manifest recording bytes + checksums. Everything else reads capsules locally; the network is touched once.

### THE SQUAD — six deterministic scouts (zero LLM, zero tokens)

These are the "signal-source agents," each reading the rep log and writing exactly one derived file. They practice **division of labor with zero duplication** — each explicitly declines the others' signal.

**The Goalkeeper (`oura_coach.mjs`) — the medical bench.** Reads Oura biometrics (~45 days, 8 endpoints) and sets today's cognitive-load ceiling: **GREEN / AMBER / RED** plus a work-type (encode/synthesise/retrieve). It is a **data-analyst, never a prescriber** — this is the hardest boundary in the system (§12). GREEN is the default; the grind is honored. RED fires only on a rare multi-day convergence of *high-confidence* signals (sleep-architecture collapse + one more), never on a single bad night. Old engine frozen verbatim as `analyzeLegacy` beside the new one (the layering law).

**The Time-Auditor (`timeaudit.mjs`) — the possession stats.** Reads ActivityWatch and classifies the day into Learning / Building / Meta minutes — the objective ground-truth of where time actually went, independent of what he *felt* he did.

**FSRS (`fsrs.mjs`) — the fitness coach.** Uses the vetted `ts-fsrs` library (FSRS-6, deterministic) to decide *which concepts need review today before they decay*. Confidence maps to spaced-repetition ratings (knew→Easy, shaky→Good, guessed→Hard, wrong→Again). It answers **WHEN**.

**Calibration (`calibration.mjs`) — the honesty auditor.** Computes **ECE** (Expected Calibration Error) — the gap between stated confidence and real accuracy — and a **danger zone** of topics he's *confident-but-wrong* about (the "knew + wrong" cell, an unknown-unknown). It answers **HOW HONEST**. It stays silent below 20 reps ("an early false alarm is worse than a missed one").

**Nemesis (`nemesis.mjs`) — the opposition scout.** Names the ONE recurring *kind of thinking* (a cross-concept fault-line cluster) that keeps beating him — a self-scout report, never a shame-list. It answers **WHAT PATTERN**. It deliberately skips signals the other scouts own (e.g. recurrence-of-guessing is FSRS's job). Healed weaknesses become trophies, kept as history.

**Learning-State (`learning_state.mjs`) — the tactics board ("the Maidan").** Maps *where he stands* on every concept (a fluency ladder: 🔴learning → 🟡held → 🟢fluent, where fluent means *cold and fast*) and *which way he's moving* (improving/holding/stalling/regressing). Draws the Maidan pitch of stages and their weak connection (the "frayed pass"). It answers **WHERE + TRAJECTORY**. It obeys the **Nidhi boundary**: it tracks outcomes and state, never *how* he learns (the pedagogy is off-limits to it).

### THE ORGANISM ORGANS (built on top of the squad)

**`physio.mjs` — proprioception.** The body sensing itself: detects bleeds (a rep uncaptured, a signal emitted-but-unconsumed, a mirror gone stale) and keeps the **speak-gates** — the volume thresholds that keep premium signals silent until enough blood has flowed. Exception-only: it says nothing unless something bleeds. (On Day 0 it correctly flagged `effort_uncaptured` — hours of learning, zero reps pasted yet.)

**`twin.mjs` — the book on the captain.** A predictive model that seals honest daily bets (will he touch the floor by 09:30? will a session happen?) *before* any day-data lands. **The constitutional law: the book only speaks when he wins.** A fitted rival you always lose to is "a whip wearing a tachometer's badge" — so the derby is voiced only in the direction he wins; when the book beats him, it resolves *silently* into scheduling. You never lose to a machine out loud. Gated behind 30 scored resolutions + beating the base rate.

**`touchline.mjs` — the sideline read.** Senses the day's shape (tunnel/wall-minutes, productive-struggle vs spinning, the tank of due work, weak-foot deferrals) and acts *only through the next packet* — it can lighten or reframe tomorrow's drills but may **never add work mid-day and never pings.** "Productive struggle" is coded to mean *do nothing.* The ear is exiled from his private confessional forever.

**`setpiece.mjs` — the set-piece coach.** Compiles tomorrow's **≤3 drills** from today's exact failures, in the interview's own probe grammar. **Drill #1 is winnable by law** (a green concept). A RED body day collapses this to one five-minute floor-touch. Now also tags each drill **voice-first or screen-first** (modality routing) so the Dugout knows which to conduct aloud.

**`scorer.mjs` — the evening scorer (the Slip).** One ledger, three books scored by one shared arithmetic (`ece()`): the captain's own proposals, the Twin's bets, the Gaffer's moves. Produces **trust tiers** — proven proposal-types earn a "no-look pass" (they shrink to one-liners on the sheet). This is the mechanism where *the sheet gets shorter as the system gets more proven* — the inverse of every tool he's abandoned.

**`scout.mjs` — the advance scout.** Threshold-staging (prepares a mock or a milestone the moment a trigger is met) and the LEARN/RATIFY edge split. **Constitutional law in code: no projected date field exists in the schema.** It stages readiness; it never shows a countdown.

**`bootroom.mjs` — the genome.** The one organ allowed to change *how he learns* — but only through **serial, pre-registered, auto-reverting mutations**, one gene at a time, each gated by his explicit approval and a measurable metric. If a mutation fails its own metric, it auto-reverts. Medical rules and honest-framing rules are constitutionally *outside* the genome's reach.

**`doubtminer.mjs` — the doubt engine.** Mines his 112 real captured confusions into wrong-prior shape-clusters, extracts his own anchor-metaphors verbatim (Ghar-ki-Boli), and runs the **Tape Room**: a past version of him returns as the cross-examiner ("Week-N you argued X — dismantle him"). A clean win *retires* the doubt (a counter that climbs beside matches-played) and mints exactly the interview material that wins offers.

**`postmatch.mjs` — the evening ledger.** The 30-second full-time ritual: HIT/MISS, one signal, and the **KAL-line** (tomorrow's pre-decided first move, in his own words — the weld that beats the morning re-entry tax). Increments matches-played (conscious rest counts as a won day, by the outwork law), routes throw-ins, and triggers the evening scoring. **No-shame is enforced:** a miss is "data, not a verdict"; the strings "fail/streak" never appear.

### THE MANAGER (`manager.mjs`) — the capstone

The gaffer who reads every scout's report and writes today's **team sheet** — but **only ever proposes.** Its architecture is the purest expression of the constitution:

- **The socket:** `runManager({llm})` takes the language model as an *injected function*. Without one, it always falls back to a deterministic skeleton — so the sheet appears **unconditionally**, even cold on Matchday 1.
- **The zero-invented-numbers validator:** the wrapper computes the complete set of legitimate numbers from the data, then *rejects any LLM output containing a number that isn't in that set.* An LLM sheet claiming "73% Building" (a number nowhere in the data) is physically rejected and the deterministic skeleton ships instead. The LLM *cannot* smuggle a hallucinated statistic past the code. This is "AI proposes · code validates · human approves" made mechanical — not a policy, a code path with a passing test.

### THE BRAIN (`brain.mjs`) — the hot, two-engine runtime

The deterministic scheduler; the LLM is only ever the passenger. **21 jobs** across the day and night (the morning formation-read that writes the sheet, the day cartridge, the two team-talks, the doubt-cluster miner, the wall painter, the poster, the season review, the metamorphosis digest, and more).

- **The budget governor.** Every call logs its true token usage. The Max plan's real limits are unpublished, so the config carries conservative estimates that **self-tune** — on any observed limit event it records the true ceiling and re-fits. It **protects study hours** (spends ≤25% of headroom 09:00–21:00 so he can always open Claude and work) and **exhausts the plan overnight** (queue-drain to ~95% — "unused capacity is wasted sharpness").
- **The third pool.** Live-voice minutes are a third budget beside Claude-window and Gemini-text; a heavy voice day defers daytime Gemini jobs so the voice keeps the free pool.
- **Honest-frame validators in code:** no brain job may emit "10x / exponential / on steroids" (banned-phrase check) or a number absent from its input (zero-invented-numbers, reused from the Manager).

## 10. THE DUGOUT — the crown (real-time voice)

`dugout.mjs` (1,010 lines) is a local bridge that serves a browser page connecting mic ⇄ **Gemini Live (free tier)** ⇄ speakers, with the Gaffer's constitution and the captain's *measured cognitive fingerprint* as the system instruction. Sub-second, interruptible, all-day.

**The JARVIS scar-table, solved empirically.** The build applied a hard-won bug-table from a prior project — but *verified every claim against the live wire* (12 Jul) rather than trusting it:
- speech config + response modalities must be **nested inside `generationConfig`** (the "root-level" claim gets a 1007 close — the wire overruled the table).
- **Charon** is the Gaffer's voice identity (verified end-to-end).
- Local **VAD** (voice-activity detection) + connect-on-voice + park-on-idle — an always-on socket hemorrhages tokens.
- Native-rate audio out, real barge-in (interrupt mid-sentence), 15-minute session-resumption stitching, key-pool rotation across free projects.
- The **mic bug** was root-caused live: the browser held the permission in state *denied*, and Chrome never re-prompts a denied origin — the page now names this before you start and surfaces every error with its fix.

**The 15 live tools** reach into the bus mid-sentence: `get_today`, `get_tape_room`, `retire_doubt`, `log_reps` (voice reps through the *real* capture contract — gut-word before answer, enforced), `take_note`, `get_calibration`, `checkpoint`, `run_postmatch`, `approve_genome`, `route_throwins`, `get_rejirah`, `set_reminder`, `ratify_interruption`, `scrimmage_report`, `semantic_recall`.

**What you can do by voice** (built across this session's U1–U4):
- **Spoken gates** — full-time, genome approval, and throw-in routing all happen on your explicit word ("haan, chalao"). *No word, no write* — the machine may never call a gate tool from its own inference.
- **The oral scrimmage** — `?mode=scrimmage` flips it into examiner conditions: one of three personas (Recruiter Ghost / Scenario Bomb / Code Autopsy), 5 timed probes, one real interruption, graded /25. A **hedge-density ear** counts your "shayad/I-think/matlab" off-mic — the one place measurement is allowed, because you *asked* to be judged.
- **The touchline eyes** — point the camera at your paper (whiteboard mode) or share your screen (commentator mode); it coaches your working live.
- **The Re-Jirah conductor** — it runs your due reviews aloud, gut-word first.
- **His-voice reminders** — "remind me at 15:00 to…" comes back in your own words at that time, once. Gate-exempt (your voice echoed is not a ping).
- **Earned proactivity** — the shadow engine (§ below) decides what it may say unprompted.
- **The day thread** — KICKOFF / GROUND / FULL-TIME phases stitch one conversation across the whole day; GROUND is bias-to-silence (it speaks only at stoppages you declare).
- **Semantic recall** — "when did I last mention X" searches your own past words (embeddings + cosine, free tier).
- **The metamorphosis loop** — every conversation transcript is mined overnight into tomorrow's curriculum. *Talking is training.*

**The shadow engine (`shadow.mjs`) — earned proactivity, the "crown mechanism."** The proactive mouth trains *silently*: every would-have-spoken moment is logged as a shadow (never voiced), and the evening scores each one ("would it have helped?"). An interruption-type earns a voice only at a **proven hit-rate (≥10 shadows, ≥0.7) plus your one-time spoken ratification.** Ratification is revoked if the proof decays. RED body day = the proactive mouth is muted entirely. This is the no-look-pass machinery pointed at the mouth: the machine must *earn the right to interrupt you.*

## 11. THE FORGE — the learning method the machine serves

The organism is the *loop*; **the FORGE is the pedagogy inside it.** "FORGE" is a blacksmithing metaphor, not an acronym: raw concept = cold steel; you heat it, hammer it, **weld** the cracks, **temper** it, rack it for re-heating. One concept at a time, painfully slow allowed, correctness never traded.

- **The per-concept pipeline (0→11):** time-box → **Daraar-map** shown (the 9 fault-lines = a visible finish line) → **Pehle-Guess** (cold pre-teaching guess) → explain → show + interactive widget → work together → alone → **Bolo** (speak it aloud, then transcribe) → **calibrate** (self-rate before the grilling) → **Jirah** (Claude as skeptical interviewer) → **Lock** (write the immutable capsule) → **Re-Jirah** (spaced re-weld at ~3 days / 2 weeks / 6 weeks).
- **The 9-axis Daraar-map (a–i):** what-it-is + analogy · why / first-principles · mechanism (name it) · math + range · limits / failure modes · trade-offs · the FinOps build-hook · scale / cost / prod gotcha · explain-3-ways (CEO / junior / skeptical senior). Every concept is stress-tested along all nine.
- **The confidence ontology** is the honesty primitive: the gut-word is committed *before* grading, because that's the only way to detect an unknown-unknown, and because *hypercorrection needs the collision* — a confidently-wrong belief, once broken, is corrected far more durably.
- **A capsule** is the durable, byte-perfect knowledge unit for one concept (4 currently locked: tokenization, embeddings, inference, context). Capsules are immutable; they carry his own words (`bolo`) verbatim, his real confusions (`doubts[]`), and interview material — governed by a **cold-reader standard** (reconstructable by future-him 6–12 months later with zero session memory).

**Why this belongs in the pitch:** the machine isn't a generic tutor. It serves a specific, deep, research-grounded method — spaced repetition, the generation effect, calibration training, retrieval practice with personalized distractors — and the whole apparatus exists to feed *that*.

## 12. The laws & the soul (what makes it a coach, not a whip)

The vision ran **three adversarial reviewers** whose only job was to kill mechanisms that betrayed the human center. What survived is the soul — and it is defended more fiercely than any feature:

- **Win-only voicing** — the predictive book speaks only when he wins; it loses silently.
- **The cold-start gag** — no prediction is ever voiced on thin data ("a morning prophecy of failure to a shame-spiral brain is the initiation wall with a probability stapled to it").
- **No shame, no streaks, no hype** — misses are data; progress is weekly-consistency %, never a streak tally; the system "never sells exponential" — "the honest frame is compounding; the ceiling is biology (reps × time × sleep)." A self-bench rule: if the coach ever performs *over* the truth, it benches itself.
- **The wall, seen without shame** — the initiation wall is detected from outside and answered by *silently lowering the door* (a 2-minute opener), never a ping, never a beep. Wall-minutes surface only as a weekly trend, never a daily meter, never on bad days.
- **The medical boundary (hard, non-negotiable)** — the Goalkeeper never comments on medication, dose, or diagnosis. Because he is medicated, heart-rate/HRV/temperature are *low-confidence, medication-confounded* signals that can never drive a verdict alone. Sustained concerning physiology → a **doctor-referral flag, full stop.** Any mood/agitation signal → "show your doctor" (never self-interpreted). **The Governor is the one organ exempt from every league table and every mutation** — "a safety brake that can be relegated by a score will one day be off when the crash comes."
- **No calendar pressure** — "no projected date is ever shown to you. Projection steers what the loop prepares, never what you owe." Pace is the captain's department; the machine never says "time is short."
- **The contested organ, left to humans** — wiring his wife in as a rater ("The Twelfth Player") is deliberately *flagged, not built* — "a decision for two humans, not one document."

**This is the moat, stated plainly:** every seductive capability — a fitted rival, a deadline, a shame-metric, a wife-as-sensor, a medical opinion — was deliberately killed or clamped in adversarial review to protect one human's psychology and safety. It is an AI system whose deepest conviction is knowing what it must refuse to do.

## 17. The tech stack (one glance)

- **Runtime:** Node 22, ESM, Windows. 27 deterministic `.mjs` organs. Atomic writes (temp→rename), empty-safe, entry-guarded.

- **AI:** Claude via `claude -p` on a Max subscription (judgment); Gemini via free CLI + free Live API (vision, bulk, voice). **No API keys, ever** — a code-enforced cost ceiling.

- **Voice:** Gemini Live (Charon) in the browser; neural TTS (`msedge-tts`, free) as the bench; local VAD.
- **Deps:** just two npm packages (`ts-fsrs` for the vetted scheduler, `msedge-tts` for the voice). Everything else is standard library.
- **Verification:** 26 self-test suites, all green; a live end-to-end pass this session that found and fixed four real automation bugs.
